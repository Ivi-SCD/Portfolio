About me

On this page you will know a little more about me and my story...

Who am i?

My name is Ivisson Pereira and since I was 14 years old, I have been studying and immersing myself in this wonderful world of programming.

However, this passion started long before I was 14, it was born with the curiosity to discover how games work (the world I was immersed in since I was 6 years old).

Present

I've been focusing on studying Java and dedicating myself to improving my skills in this language, particularly with the Spring Boot framework and its components, as well as other tools involved in software development.

Additionally, I'm also learning front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with the aim of specializing in the React library and the Angular framework.

Hobbies

Shifting gears a bit, I have a strong passion for studying the financial market and business, and enjoy sharing my knowledge and engaging in discussions with others on current market-related issues.

Additionally, I love reading horror books, with my favorite author being Edgar Allan Poe and my favorite co-author being Stephen King. Since childhood, I have had a passion for gaming, particularly on PC, with indie games being my preferred genre. However, at present, I find myself playing League of Legends the most.

Lastly, I have a keen interest in traveling and exploring new places.

First Contacts

My first experience with programming was with Python. Its simple syntax immediately captured my attention and inspired me to dive deeper into the language.

As I gained a better understanding of the fundamentals, I began to explore more complex projects using the Django framework. While I took a break from studying programming for a few months, I eventually returned and continued to broaden my knowledge with other languages such as C and C++. However, I found that Java resonated with me the most and it has become my primary focus of study.

Professional Goal

I have numerous professional goals and aspirations that I hope to achieve throughout my career.

However, my primary focus at present is to become a Software Architect and work collaboratively with the technology industry to make a positive impact on people's lives and enhance their overall comfort.
